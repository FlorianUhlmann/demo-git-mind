## [0.1.1] — 2026-04-09

**Conceptual sharpening.** Corrected framing to center on thinker-to-thinker synthesis; introduced Transition Tax and The Loop as named core concepts.

### Changed
- `CONTEXT.md` — core argument reframed: not human-machine, but synthesis between thinkers; added Transition Tax and The Loop as key concepts; updated Discovery Problem description to emphasize research trajectory
- `sections/02_problems.md` — Problem 2.1 expanded: Transition Tax named and defined, the broken loop (thinking → documenting) made explicit; Problem 2.2 updated: flat surface / top-to-bottom reading contrasted with GitHub navigation model
- `sections/05_collaboration.md` — framing adjusted: AI as infrastructure, not primary collaborator; Speed of Thought explicitly tied to eliminating Transition Tax between thinkers

### Why
Direct feedback from the author: the original text over-indexed on human-AI collaboration and under-explained why chat output stays locked — the transition cost, not the model, is the core problem.
